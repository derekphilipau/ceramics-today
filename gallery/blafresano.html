 Indian Maiden
First this piece was glazed where silver was going to be applied.
Different colors of glazes also on necklaces and then fired.
Then after firing silver was applied  to ear rings and jewelry and fired
again. Then painted and dry-brushed. Then antiqued with fashion hues
and oil. Finally chalked and then sprayed off
