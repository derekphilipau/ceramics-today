Yakimono

Cement, Oklahoma, USA
studiotoki@aol.com
http://members.aol.com/studiotoki/toki0001.htm

Leather Enchantment Collection, Bound Up My Treasure

Dimension: 4" x 6"
Technique: Stoneware, Oxidation, Leather
For Sale: $75.00

Artist Statement: Pottery is a vision of human creativity, with works of art which have maintained their form and glaze color for thousands of years. I use clay bodies to express visions of mind and spirit. In my work, I design vessels through creative expression of the hand, developing unusual and appealing art with carefully designed glazes and often adding driftwood and leather to give the piece a natural appeal. Using my own glaze designs, each piece depicts the powerful effects of glaze materials interacting with fire. Many of my pieces are of Japanese, African and Native American Indian creation. My desire is to have those who view my pottery to feel the warmth and spirit in each piece.
