Kenton Baker

Strasburg, PA, USA
kentonbkr@hotmail.com

Double Necked Vase

Dimensions: 13 1/2" H x 5 1/2"W
Firing: unglazed stoneware, wood fired in an Anagama kiln

I have been firing exclusively with wood as fuel and building wood fired kilns for many years. I strive to get the rich colors and effects of the flame on each piece I make. My goal is to use the wood firing process along with local clays and clay mixtures to give each piece of work a sense of time and place which reflect on the artist's growth within a given time and place.
