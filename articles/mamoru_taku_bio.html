Artist's Profile  

Mamoru Taku
Biography

2003:   "Large Bizen Plate With Fire Marks", selected for the "50th Exhibition of Japanese Traditional Art Crafts"
(Museum of Japanese Traditional Art Crafts)
2002: Work selected for the Exhibition of "Fine Art in Tea Ceremony"
1991: Prize-winning work exhibited at the exhibition by "Tou Shin Kai"
1990: Work selected for the exhibition of "Japanese Traditional Art Crafts in Chugoku Area"
1989: Work selected for the Exhibition of "Fine Art in Tea Ceremony" and "Japanese Traditional Art Crafts in Chugoku Area"
1988: Work selected for the Exhibition of "Asahi Art Crafts" and "Japanese TraditionalArt Crafts"
1987: Work selected for "Okayama Prefecture Art Exhibition" and "Japanese Traditional Art Crafts in Chugoku Area"
1986: Work selected for the "International Ceramics Exhibition of Japan and China", "Okayama Prefecture Art Exhibition" and "Japanese Traditional Art Crafts in Chugoku Area"
1985: Prize-winning work exhibited at "Okayama Prefecture Art Exhibition" and "Japanese Traditional Art Crafts in Chugoku Area"
1984: Work selected for the "International Ceramics Exhibition of Japan and China" and Okayama Prefecture Art Exhibition"
1983: Work selected for "The Exhibition of "the 7th Ceramics Art In Japan" and "Japanese Traditional Art Crafts in Chugoku Area"

1982: Work selected for "Okayama Prefecture Art Exhibition" and "Japanese Traditional Art Crafts In Chugoku Area"
1980: Started pottery as an assistant at Takaaki Kimura
1952: Born in Okayama Prefecture, Japan
