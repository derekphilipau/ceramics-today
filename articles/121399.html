People's Choice Award

Dateline: 12/13/99

nnouncing the People's Choice Best Ceramics Web Site Award. Win a yearly subscription to Ceramics Art & Perception or Ceramics Technical by taking part!

We all know that there are web sites - and there are web sites. Calling on the spirit of good competition - and promoting good design in online ceramics, your Ceramics Today Guide to ceramics has created this new award for best ceramics web site. We are not looking for the best ceramic work, but rather the best design representing that work online. The competition is open to all web sites listed on Ceramics Today, such as individual potters, potteries and ceramic societies, commercial sites, galleries and educational institutions. In fact any of the categories listed on the Ceramics Today main page will qualify.

What are the criteria for good web design? Here are some:

  • user friendliness (easy navigation)
  • good design concept
  • good graphics
  • fast loading
  • informative

Of course you may apply other criteria, or one of the above may outweigh the other, e.g. if you like the design so much it overrides difficulties in navigation.

Following five categories that will be judged and awarded a gold, silver and bronze 'medal', according to the number of votes received:

  • individual artists or artists potteries
  • ceramic society or educational institution
  • commercial site
  • gallery or museum
  • miscellaneous

The only condition is that the site be listed on Ceramics Today,or be submitted to be listed on the database.

Voting has ended. Please keep your eyes on the main page for the winners to be announced soon!
