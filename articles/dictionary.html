Submissions sought for Dictionary of Ceramic Art and Artists

If you are a ceramic artist or potter of demonstrable international repute or a major ceramic artist or potter in your country, then Steven Goldate would like to hear from you! Steven is writing a Dictionary of Ceramic Art and Artists and would like your input. If you think you fulfill the above criteria, please email your CV and a short description of your work to .

No guarantee is given for inclusion in the directory!

Please note: You must have a demonstrable international, or strong national reputation, otherwise your submission will be disregarded. Indications for your suitability are: you have had several national and/or international exhibitions; you have participated numerous group shows; your work has been written up in any of the major ceramics magazines; you have been invited to speak at national or international ceramics festivals; you are a ceramics teacher on a tertiary level; you have been artist-in-residence at international locations; your work has been collected by major public institutions; your work is well-known internationally for other reasons. Your CV should include: your year of birth; if, when and where you studied; a description of your work including materials, firing processes etc.; national & international awards; your influences (if any); professional memberships; website address; other major achievements; other activities, etc.
