The Clay Studio of Missoula presents: SCENE new work by Minerva Chango

3-26 October 2003

Join Archie Bray Summer Resident Minerva Chango on 4 October 2003, 10 am at the Clay Studio of Missoula for an “altered form” workshop and slide presentation. Minerva Chango, a professor of ceramics in Caracas, Venezuela, has studied with Jeff Oestreich and worked in clay for over fifteen years. Her thrown and altered forms combine a strong architectural line with a sense of animated whimsy.

For more information, or to reserve a space, please call Jayson Lawfer at 543-0509.

The Clay Studio of Missoula a non-profit facility for the ceramic arts at 910 Dickens, Missoula, Montana, USA 59801. Phone (406) 543-0509.
