Global Arts Village Fellowship.

 

Unfortunately I have received numerous bad reports about this residency. Here are a few excerpts (artists' names withheld):

 

"Myself and other artists were subject to mental bullying by being excluded from the main area where we lived. We were bossed around to do all kinds of menial tasks like gardening in the mid day sun because westerners were spoilt and needed there ego breaking down, we were told if we did not do these jobs we would be sent home, despite paying to be there. There is also a sinister undertone to the whole place because Ashwin, the man who runs it, is OSHO and tries to impose his Osho ideas on artists who stay at the village."

"I am sorry to inform you that in my opinion the GAV is a racketeer. the problems have been numerous, from the rooms flooding on a near constant basis without being addressed. Services advertised and extra funds paid for are supplied haphazardly. There has been allegations of sexual harassment on past and present residencies.It appears to target young women. There were initially 5 artists all commencing on 04.08.06. All artists have vacated GAV with no refund, not even half way through our residencies!

I received a termination notice at 7.30pm 15.09.06 whilst in hospital my baby son who traveled with me has required 2 major operations during our short stay, GAV was aware of timing of 2nd operation so motives appear to be premeditated and callous. There has been no reason given for my termination, I had to engage to help of the police to have my belongings returned. My son and I have been basically rendered homeless in a foreign country no refund for my $6127 USD has been returned.The place seems to be run on fear and intimidation I and other artists have received veiled threats. I have managed to contact previous residents and have received approx 20 emails of similar horrific stories. The owner has claimed that GAV is an Ashram and a spiritual place which follows the osho practice (I have discovered Osho is the new name for the Rajneshi - orange people cult). I am absolutely horrified that this type of treatment has been rendered onto artists on an ongoing basis although complaints and recommendations have been made to directly GAV in the past.

I WOULD NOT BE ABLE TO RECCOMMEND GAV TO ANYONE, and would suggest any organization affiliated with them make further enquiries before being associated with GAV. I found GAV through Res artis and Alliance. Res artis have received numerous complaints along the line as mine and re no longer associated with them."

"After only a day I was approached by Ashwin, who asked me to come to his room where he would give me a massage. An incident that is not appropriate on any level to a new artist. From that point on I felt very uncomfortable in my new living situation and felt I must stay away from him. This pattern continued with other members of the staff. I feel since I am a young girl they could try and take advantage of me. On another occasion we had a "welcome celebration" for the artists to highlight their work, I did not feel that it was about our artistic endeavors whatsoever. That night Sunil was talking to me and started asking me very inappropriate questions. It started with just information about my family, how they are, how long my parents have been together and so forth. I feel he was using this information to see how much he could ask me. He then proceeded to ask me if I was a virgin, something that even people close to me would not dare ask, nor is it anyone's business. After this uncomfortable talk I wanted to leave and go back to my room, since I was the only person there and I was feeling very threatened. He then gave me a hug and proceeded to kiss me, I pulled away and he again started kissing me on my neck, I even said I did not feel comfortable. I went to bed that night feeling terrible and mistreated and in the morning I was extremely upset when thinking what happened. On September 20th, another situation occurred where I had a terrible headache and I asked Aruto for some medicine, he gave me some and then told me that he could release the tension in my head by a simple technique. Since he is a certified masseuse I trusted that it would help, but when rubbing my head he pulled me close and tried to kiss me, I pulled away, uncomfortable, got up and left. He apologized and I just walked out. These are just my personal incidents of this nature, there have also been many more situations that I feel the artists have been mistreated."

"The problems encountered there have been numerous and quite horrific in detail. To cut a long story short all of the artists including myself have left after only 5 weeks. No refund of any form has been made by GAV to anyone. I have received emails from other former residents stating very similar stories ranging from sexual harassment to veiled threats and intimidation. I am taking legal action against GAV. I think its important to prevent this type of scam from happening to any further artists who have worked very hard to visit India."
