Statement

   
Mitsuru Cope

Have been working in ceramics since 1981 and have exhibited nationally and internationally since 1988. My techniques, forms and ideas have changed and taken many different directions over the years. Recently, have been working with ceramic paper, using a technique I developed myself to produce a flexible, thin porcelain paper which can be folded or cut just like ordinary paper. After firing, the paper becomes a translucent ceramic object.
