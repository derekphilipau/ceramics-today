Test your knowledge!

December 2005 quiz.

Answers.

  1. Which country was Bernhard Leach born in? China (Hong Kong)
  2. Who made the so-called 'Scarab Vase'? Adelaide Alsop Robineau
  3. What is its correct title? Apotheosis of the Toiler
  4. In which country was Europe's first (soft-paste) porcelain formulated? Italy
  5. What is the opacifying ingredient in 'maiolica' (or 'majolica')? Tin oxide
  6. Which controversial British artist-potter won the 2003 Turner prize? Grayson Perry
  7. In which French town did Picasso make most of his ceramic works? Vallauris
  8. Supplement the surnames of these famous emigré ceramists: Lucie R.; Hans C.; Eva Z. Rie, Coper, Zeisel
  9. In which country did Raku originate? Japan
  10. Which British pottery manufacturer made a series of copies of the famous Roman Portland Vase? Wedgwood
