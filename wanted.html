Submit an Article to
Ceramics Today

Ceramics Today is an international and free resource for ceramics enthusiasts from around the world.

It is currently read by more than 30,000 people every month. The site is run on a voluntary basis.

Do you have an interesting article you would like to share with the world ceramics community? Many generous writers have contributed in the past, including Edouard Bastarache, Helen Bates, Tove Jespersen, Robert Yellin, Ceramics Art & Perception magazine and many others.

Artists and authors are welcome to submit written material to Ceramics Today for possible inclusion as a feature article. Articles need not be especially written for CT, but should be of general interest to the online ceramics community. Please email any article suggestions to the Editor, . You, the author, retain full copyright to the material and can request its removal at any time.

Thanks for considering a contribution to this resource!
