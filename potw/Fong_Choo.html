Featured Artist  

Fong Choo
Singapore-born American studio potter

Fong Choo is a Singapore-born American studio potter and teacher making miniature sculptural teapots, some no larger than an egg. He stablished a studio in Louisville, Kentucky in 1985.

 

Choo graduated from Warren Wilson College in Swannanoa, North Carolina in 1988, and in 1993 earned a BA in Business and Art at University of Louisville, Kentucky. He has been Adjunct Professor and artist-in-residence at Bellarmine University, Louisville since 1990.

 

Fong Choo's awards include numerous national ‘Best in Shows’ and ‘Awards of Excellence’, e.g. at the Gasparilla Festival of Arts in Tampa Florida and the American Craft Exposition in Evanston, Ilinois.
