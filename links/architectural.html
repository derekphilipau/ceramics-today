We specialize in architectural ceramic sculpture. Much of our work includes landscape design, incorporating artworks in timber, steel and stone.

Jones, Sharon

Peter King established the Stonehaus Architectural Ceramics studio in Pensacola, Florida in 1977 and has since developed a unique style of ornamental architectural ceramics.

Latka, Tom and Jean
