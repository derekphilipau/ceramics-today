Forms and Shapes: The Narrative Vessel

Gold Coast International Ceramic Art Award
An international competition with over $20,000 (AUD) in Awards, collection and prize money. Conducted annually on the Gold Coast in Queensland Australia.

Gouda Pottenbakkers Festival
The Dutch city of Gouda is not only known for its cheese - it also hosts an annual international potter's festival and competition. Amongst the events is the 'throwing the tallest vase' competition. Multilingual site.

Ceramics festival held every two years in Aberystwyth, Wales. Demonstrations, lectures, slideshows, exhibitions, films etc.

Potfest
Putting public and potters together in large numbers, to promote the sale of ceramics currently being produced in small workshops throughout the UK and mainland Europe.

Past (but still of interest):

Jerwood Applied Arts Prize 2001: Ceramics
The Jerwood Charitable Foundation and the Crafts Council will award the Jerwood Applied Arts Prize 2001 for Ceramics. A £15,000 prize will be awarded to the entrant who, in the opinion of the jury, has made the most significant contribution to contemporary ceramics in the UK. Their work should demonstrate commitment, excellence and innovation over the past five years.
