Robert Compton Pottery
A working pottery for 30 years, Noborigama wood fired kiln, Raku, Pit Firing, Salt Glazed Stoneware and Reduction Fired Stoneware. Hosting Summer Workshops for intermediate and advanced potters. Thrown and altered forms, one of a kind pots fountains and stoneware aquariums. Located in Bristol, Vermont, USA.

Houses nearly 8,000 ceramic and glass objects, ranging from small pottery shards recovered from ancient civilizations to contemporary sculpture and installation pieces to advanced ceramics reflecting the cutting edge of ceramic technology. A teaching and research facility, one educational component of the New York State College of Ceramics at Alfred University.
