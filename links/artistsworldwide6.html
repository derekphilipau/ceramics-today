McClure, Mavis
Mavis McClure is considered by many to be one of the finest figurative artists to emerge in the past decade. Self taught and just 33, McClure’s larger-than-life size figures are dominated by exaggerated hands and feet, reminiscent, but not imitative, of both Cycladic sculpture and Picasso’s versions thereof.

Pioneer Australian studio potter. Moon has been a ceramist for over fifty years and has been both lecturer and practising potter.He is the recipient of a Winston Churchill Foundation Fellowship, is a Myer Foundation Geijutsu Fellow and a Member of the Order of Australia.
